🚀 Quick-Commerce Sales Optimization (Zepto)

🧠 Business Problem
A fast-growing quick-commerce company like Zepto is facing:
Heavy use of discounts reducing profit margins
Lack of clarity on high-performing products
Uncertainty around influencer marketing effectiveness

👉 Key Question:
How can the company maximize revenue while controlling discounts?

⚙️ Approach
🔹 Data Preparation
Cleaned dataset using Python (Pandas)
Handled missing values & duplicates
Created new features:
Discount % = (Original - Current) / Original
Revenue per Order

🔹 Analysis
Focused on business-driven insights:
Identified categories with high revenue but heavy discounts
Analyzed city-wise performance
Evaluated influencer impact on revenue & orders
Detected top-selling vs low-profit products

🔹 Visualization (Power BI Dashboard)
Built an interactive dashboard using Power BI:
KPI Cards → Total Revenue, Avg Discount %, Orders
Bar Charts → Revenue by Category & City
Scatter Plot → Discount vs Revenue
Comparison → Influencer vs Non-Influencer

💡 Key Insights
High discounts do not always increase revenue
Certain categories generate higher revenue with lower discounts
Influencer campaigns improve orders but not always profitability
Few cities contribute major share of revenue

📈 Business Recommendations
Reduce discounts on low-performing products
Focus on high-margin categories
Use influencers selectively for high ROI campaigns
Optimize pricing strategy based on city-level performance

Python (Data Cleaning & Analysis)
SQL (Data Querying)
Power BI (Dashboard + DAX)
Business Analysis & Storytelling
